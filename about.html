Who am I?

My name is Christoffer Skytte Wielsøe.
I was born in the year of the pig in 1995 in Køge but I now live in Copenhagen.

I enjoy many styles of art. At the moment I have a deep interest in learning about far-eastern designs. I love their use of negative space and minimalism.
Xu Beihong's famous horse painting is one I admire alot.

Another passion of mine is sports. Specifically, baseball and basketball. I had played basketball for more than 11 years when I quit. I got to play in the second best league in Denmark at the age of 19.
Now I enjoy playing baseball with one of only five teams in Denmark, the Urban Achievers. Last season, we were able to win the national championship for the fifth consecutive year.
